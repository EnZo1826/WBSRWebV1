Vision

Walvis Bay Salt Holdings will be a world class, fully vertically integrated producer of high quality sea salt products by 2020.

Innovation

We continuously seek opportunities to deliver more value in a better, faster and more cost-effective way.

Customer Success

We continuously strive to help our internal and external customers become more successful in what they do. We always put our customers first.

Accountability

We accept our individual and team responsibility and we meet our commitments. We take responsibility for our performance in all our decisions and actions.

Respect

We treat our environment, workplace, colleagues, customers and suppliers with respect and sensitivity. We seek sustainable business practices and act as responsible citizens of the community where we work and live.

Excellence


We strive to become the best in the world in everything we do. We believe in continuous improvement and always strive to become world class.

Safety

We believe that no task is so important or urgent that it cannot be done in a safe and environmentally friendly manner.
